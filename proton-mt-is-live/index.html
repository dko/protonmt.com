Proton MT is live and ready to produce blocks.

We made the decision early on to partner with a local state-of-the-art data center. Our main servers are located in a tier-3 data center on a backbone to the internet in the "middle of the country.”

Our commitment and vision:

Integrity

We strive to find better ways to streamline and secure our networks, faithfully serve our customers and the community while following the best business practices and models.

Tier 3 Data Center

Our main servers are located in a tier-3 data center on an internet backbone in the "middle of the country." This allows us to have power and connection redundancy. Simply put, we avoid the data bottlenecks and outages that plague most other geographical areas.

Giving back to the Proton community

We believe education is the key to mass adoption of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. To this end, our immediate focus is producing online promotional and training materials in order to show businesses how they can integrate the Proton blockchain into their workflows. This space is also for highlighting project milestones and outstanding contributions by community members. Feel free to contact us with your suggestions and ideas for adding value to the Proton community.
